Who We Are

Crafting Beauty Since 2006

Lumière was founded in Paris by Élise Beaumont and Thomas Marchand — two visionaries who believed that great design should be deeply personal, never generic.

What began as a small atelier in Le Marais has grown into an internationally recognized studio with projects spanning Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Yet our core philosophy remains unchanged: every space should tell its owner's story.

Today, our team of 35+ designers, architects, and project managers continues to push the boundaries of what interior design can achieve — creating environments that inspire, nurture, and delight.

Our Mission

To Design Spaces That Elevate Everyday Living

We believe your environment shapes your wellbeing. Our mission is to create interiors that are not only visually stunning but functionally brilliant — spaces that enhance the quality of life for everyone who enters them.

Our Vision

To Be the World's Most Trusted Design Partner

We envision a world where every space — regardless of scale or budget — benefits from thoughtful, intentional design. We're building a studio that sets the global standard for client care and creative excellence.
