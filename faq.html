General

What services does Lumière offer? +
We offer a full suite of design services including interior design, architectural design, space planning, 3D visualization, furniture curation, and project management for residential, commercial, and hospitality clients.
Where are you located? +
Our main studio is in Paris (14 Rue de la Paix, 75002), with satellite offices in London and Dubai. We serve clients globally through our virtual design service.
Do you work internationally? +
Yes, we have completed projects across 12 countries spanning Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Our E-Design service allows us to work with clients anywhere in the world.
How do I get started with Lumière? +
Simply book a free consultation through our Contact page. We'll schedule a 30-minute call to discuss your vision, requirements, and how we can help. There's no obligation — just an honest conversation about your space.

Our Process

What does your design process look like? +
Our process follows four key phases: Discovery (understanding your needs), Concept (mood boards, 3D renders), Design (detailed specifications and sourcing), and Delivery (procurement, installation, and reveal). Each phase includes client approval before moving forward.
How long does a typical project take? +
Single-room projects typically take 6–8 weeks. Full home designs span 3–6 months depending on scope and complexity. Architectural projects and new builds can take 12–18 months. We provide a detailed timeline at the proposal stage.
How involved do I need to be? +
As much or as little as you'd like. Some clients love being part of every decision; others prefer to hand over full creative control. We adapt to your preference and keep you informed through regular updates and milestone approvals.

Pricing & Payment

How much does interior design cost? +
Our projects start from €8,000 for single-room designs. Full home projects typically range from €25,000–€60,000+, depending on scope, size, and material selections. We provide a detailed fixed-price proposal before any commitment.
Are there any hidden fees? +
Never. We believe in complete transparency. Our proposal includes a detailed cost breakdown, and any changes in scope are discussed and approved before implementation. What we quote is what you pay.
What payment terms do you offer? +
We typically work with a staged payment structure: 30% at project commencement, 40% at design approval, and 30% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange custom payment schedules.

Project Details

Can you work with my existing furniture? +
Absolutely. We can integrate treasured pieces into a new design, suggest reupholstering or refinishing, or advise on what to keep and what to replace. Our goal is to create a cohesive space that works for you.
Do you manage contractors and builders? +
Yes, full project management is included in our Signature and Prestige packages. We coordinate with trusted contractors, oversee all construction work, and ensure quality standards are maintained throughout.
What if I don't like the initial concept? +
We present 2-3 concept directions and refine based on your feedback. Our collaborative process ensures we're aligned before progressing. We've never had a client unhappy with the final result — our 98% satisfaction rate speaks for itself.

Materials & Sourcing

Do you use sustainable materials? +
Yes, sustainability is core to our practice. We source eco-friendly materials wherever possible — reclaimed woods, recycled metals, organic textiles, and low-VOC finishes. We're proud to be B Corp certified.
Can I choose my own materials and furniture? +
Of course. While we provide expert recommendations and access to exclusive suppliers, you're always welcome to suggest specific pieces or brands. We'll ensure everything works harmoniously within the overall design vision.
Do you have access to exclusive brands? +
Yes, our trade partnerships give us access to over 200 exclusive brands and artisans worldwide — many of which aren't available to the general public. This includes custom furniture makers, specialist craftsmen, and luxury fabric houses.
