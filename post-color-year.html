Colour of the Year: How to Use It

Jan 28, 2024··4 min read

Color has the power to transform mood and space. This year's hero shades are all about grounding and serenity.

1. Earthy Terracotta

Use muted clay tones in bedrooms to create a nurturing, cocoon-like atmosphere that promotes rest and relaxation.

2. Deep Forest Greens

Introduce depth and connection to nature with forest greens in your study or living area for a sophisticated, calming environment.
