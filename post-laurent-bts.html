Behind the Scenes: The Laurent Residence

Feb 22, 2024··8 min read

An inside look at our transformation of a historic Parisian apartment into a modern masterpiece of light and form.

1. Preserving the Past

The original Haussmann-style moldings were carefully restored by hand to maintain the building's 19th-century soul while we updated the floor plan.

2. Modern Intervention

We introduced custom Italian joinery and contemporary lighting to contrast with the period details, creating a sophisticated dialogue between eras.
