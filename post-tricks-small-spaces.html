7 Designer Tricks for Small Spaces

March 8, 2024··5 min read

Limited square footage doesn’t mean limited style. Our designers share their secrets for making any space feel grand.

1. Mirrored Illusions

Strategic mirror placement can bounce light and effectively double the visual depth of a room. Use floor-to-ceiling mirrors in narrow hallways for maximum impact.

2. Vertical Thinking

When floor space is scarce, look up. Built-in shelving and floor-to-ceiling drapery draw the eye upward, creating an illusion of height.
