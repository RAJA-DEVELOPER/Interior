Villa Serene

A 280 m·² Parisian penthouse transformed into a masterpiece of modern luxury and timeless French elegance.

The Project

Timeless Parisian Elegance

The serene project approached us with a vision: transform their newly acquired penthouse in the Monaco into a home that honoured the building's Haussmann heritage while feeling distinctly contemporary.

Our design response balanced ornate period features — herringbone oak floors, decorative mouldings, marble fireplaces — with clean modern furnishings, a pared-back palette of warm neutrals, and carefully curated contemporary art.

The result is a home that feels both grand and deeply intimate — a sanctuary in the heart of Paris.
