The Service

Creating Spaces With Soul

Our interior design service is a deeply collaborative journey. We begin by understanding your lifestyle, your aesthetics, and your aspirations — then we translate them into a space that feels like home from the very first moment you walk in.

Whether it's a single signature room or a full-home transformation, every project receives the same level of care, creativity, and precision that has made Lumière a trusted name for nearly two decades.

We handle everything — from spatial planning and material selection to furniture procurement and final styling — so you can enjoy the transformation without the stress.

Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take? +
Most single-room projects take 6–8 weeks from concept to completion. Full home projects typically span 3–6 months depending on scope and complexity.
Do you work with existing furniture? +
Absolutely. We can integrate treasured pieces into a new design, reupholster and refinish items, or advise on what to keep and what to replace.
Can you work within a specific budget? +
Yes. We create a detailed budget breakdown at the start and manage spending transparently throughout the project. No hidden fees, ever.
Do you offer virtual design services? +
Yes, our E-Design package offers complete design solutions for clients anywhere in the world, delivered through high-resolution renders and detailed specification documents.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve clients across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Our Paris, London, and Dubai offices enable us to manage projects globally.
